Our History

Established in 2005

To keep domestic animals off the streets into the arms of loving families, Fluffy Ears Adoption, aimed to be a protecter of abandoned domestic pets, and strays. Giving them the life they deserve and enriching families lives with their new family friend. Raising awareness since 2005 and still going strong, in 2015, we recieved the Happy Paws reward from our local community in Lohja, Finland. But it didnt stop then, every year we strive to do better than the last. With collaboration with our partners and charities growing every year, we continue to stregthen our efforts to save lifes and create families.

Founder Konrad Szulc


Welcome, my name is Konrad Szulc. A long time ago, I disliked seeing my local neighbourhood riddled with all types of abandoned domestic animals. I was so disheartned by their treatment and how unsafe it was for them especially in nordic countries, where the weather gets too cold for them to survive. Thus I wanted to make a change, and since then have been putting my two feet forward to combat this problem.

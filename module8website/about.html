What You Need To Know

We Love Animals

We host events every month on the last sunday of the month, but our shelters are open 9-5pm for visitors. If you would like to donate, our donation box is open 24/7 outside any shelter. We are trying to make a difference, every bit helps. You are also welcome to volunteer, or work for us, please get in contact on our contact page. We currently have about 100+ employees with shelters throughout the nordic countries. Our animals have constant checks to the vets, and are vaccinated/dewormed. We strive to have them in good health and fit their specific needs. As much as we love all of them, we are happy to give them to families who love them as much as we do.
